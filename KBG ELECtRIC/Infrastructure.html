Commencement & Growth

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leadership 

KBG Electric Pvt. Ltd. began with a clear vision: to deliver dependable, custom-engineered electrical panel solutions backed by technical excellence and integrity. Over the years, we have grown steadily, evolving into a trusted brand with a strong foothold in multiple industries. 

Our journey reflects a blend of passion, expertise, and a commitment to deliver beyond expectations.

Professionally Managed. Technically Driven.

From day one, we have invested in building a team of highly skilled engineers, technicians, and support staff—each committed to quality and innovation. Our professionals bring in-depth knowledge of the electrical, electronics, and instrumentation domains, allowing us to meet even the most complex project demands with confidence.

Milestones of Growth

Expanded Facility: Grown to a 7,000 sq. ft. modern manufacturing unit Increased Output: Achieved production capacity worth ₹20+ Cr annually Diverse Portfolio: Successfully executed turnkey solutions across various sectors including power, process, HVAC, and automation Wider Reach: Built a satisfied client base across India, with repeat orders and long-term collaborations

Quality as a Core Pillar

Quality has always been the driving force behind our growth. Our in-house Quality Control Department ensures that every input material and production process goes through rigorous checks. We are committed to building panels that are reliable, safe, and compliant with relevant standards.
 
Looking Ahead With ambitious plans for expansion, KBG Electric Pvt. Ltd. aims to add new product lines, adopt smarter technologies, and further enhance its footprint across the country. Our goal is not just to grow in numbers—but to grow in trust, capability, and customer satisfaction.
