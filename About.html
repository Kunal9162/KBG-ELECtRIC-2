At KBG Electric Pvt. Ltd., we believe that excellence in engineering is built on a foundation of strong values, committed teamwork & forward-thinking innovation. Since our inception, we have focused on delivering high-quality electrical panel solutions that serve the evolving needs of industries across India.

We are not more than just manufacturers-we are partners in our client's progress. Our approach combines technical expertise, hands-on experience & a deep understanding of project demands to deliver reliable, performance-driven solutions.

Our Philosophy

We are committed to exceeding customer expectations by offering efficient, cost-effective & customized electrical control panel systems. Our business is built on long-term relationships, trust & a dedication to continuous improvement in everything we do-from design & production to service & support.

Mr. Krishna B. Gawade, Founder & CEO of KBG Electric Pvt. Ltd., brings over 2 decades of extensive experience in electrical engineering. A dynamic leader & project execution expert, he has led numerous projects in Power Distribution, HVAC, Process Industries & Automation. His commitment to quality, innovation & social responsibility shapes the company's vision & inspires the team to deliver excellence at every step.
